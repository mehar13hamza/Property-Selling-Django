About Us


We Make Real Estate Promotion Simple and Cost-Effective

One of the biggest challenges for real estate professionals and clients nowadays is the rising cost of promotion. On top of that, listing a property can be a tedious, time-consuming process involving numerous phone calls. Even buyers and renters have a hard time finding the right property in a myriad of choices. Algorithms have grown more sophisticated, yet more unreliable. CEO David Pineda founded Invexterra in 2017 to change that. With his 3 years of experience in the real estate industry, David Pineda envisioned a digital platform that would make the promotion of real estate easier and less expensive, not only for the local and national market, but the international market as well.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make real estate promotion a simple and affordable process. We do that by providing a user-friendly platform that connects buyers, sellers, and renters to do business at a reasonable cost. Invexterra removes the confusing bells and whistles to streamline the listing process. We place the needs of our clients first and foremost and work closely with each of them to devise the best strategy. We also closely follow the latest changes in the real estate market to keep users informed and help them make the best deals.

Our Values

At Invexterra, we have a set of core values we are deeply committed to. First, simplicity is at the core of our philosophy. We strive to provide a user-friendly platform that caters to the needs of each type of user. Moreover, we want our users to feel supported at all times. Creating a win-win situation in every deal. To that end, each of our team members works closely with agents, real estate companies, and clients to address their specific needs and help them reach their goals. We also follow a continuous learning process to adapt to the current real estate market. We want our users to feel like esteemed partners, not a portfolio that yields a return on autopilot.
